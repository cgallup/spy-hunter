Click the link below to start the game.

Use the left/right arrows or A/D to steer, and the space bar to fire. Press O to drop an oil slick, S to fire smoke, and X to fire a missile (smoke and missiles must be picked up by driving alongside the blue van). Press P to pause.

On mobile (iPhone/Android), use the on-screen ◀ ▶ FIRE, OIL, and SPECIAL buttons.
